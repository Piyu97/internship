What is Insta Cart?

Insta Cart is India’s largest and most convenient hyper local delivery company which enables you to order fruits and vegetables and other products that you need every day, directly via your mobile or web browser.

Do you charge any amount or taxes over and above the rates shown?

No, we do not charge anything over and above the rates shown. However, we do have a delivery fee in case the order value per store does not reach the minimum order value for free delivery.

What If I want to return something?

If you’re dissatisfied with the products delivered, please register a complaint via the app Within 72hours for non-perishable items and 24hours for perishable items. Our customer Support team will get in touch with you to resolve this issue. You can also return the products which you are dissatisfied with, at the time of delivery and we will get there fund initiated for you.
