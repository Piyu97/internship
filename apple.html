ABOUT

Apple
An apple is a sweet, edible fruit produced by an apple tree.
And it is one of the most popular fruit.Apple tree are cultivated worldwide.
They are large if grown from seed.
It's great tasting, crunchy, comes in a variety of colors and flavors, and makes a great snack.
Some popular types of apples are: Cortland, Red Delicious, Golden Delicious and many more.

Interesting Fact

Apples are low in calories.
A medium apple provides about 95 calories, is practically fat-free, contains no cholesterol or salt.
Antioxidants in apples protect nerve cells and can help prevent neurodegenerative diseases.
They also help to keep teeth and gums healthy.
Apples are a source of insoluble fiber which aids digestion.
