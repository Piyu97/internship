ABOUT

Banana
Banana is the common name for a type of fruit and also the name for the herbaceous plants that grow it.
Most banana plants are grown for their fruits and are available year-round.
They are bigger in size and bright green.
They are cut and left in large clusters just as they grew.

Interesting Fact

Bananas are great super food which provides us with energy, make us feel full and provide our body with essential nutrients and high amount of fiber.
They serve an important role in many traditional diets.
They may help prevent asthma, cancer, high blood pressure diabeties cardiovascular disease, and digestive problems.
